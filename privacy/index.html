Privacy Policy

Your data privacy is our priority.

1. No Data Collection

Stylish QR Code is a client-side application. This means that all the information you enter—whether it be URLs, Wi-Fi passwords, contact details (vCard), or SMS messages—is processed locally in your web browser.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or the content of the QR codes you generate to any external servers.

2. Local Processing

The generation of QR codes is handled by the StylishQrGen private, secure and client side JavaScript library directly on your device. Since there is no "backend" database involved in the generation process, your sensitive data never leaves your computer or smartphone.

3. Local Storage

Our application may use Local Storage or Cookies strictly for the purpose of saving your preferred UI settings (such as chosen style templates or last used colors). This is done solely to improve your user experience and is never shared with third parties.
